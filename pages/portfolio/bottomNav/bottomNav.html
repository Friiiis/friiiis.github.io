Bottom navigation bar

A concept design for a bottom navigation bar to be used in mobile apps.

Case:

Design a bottom navigation bar focused on text instead of icons. Focusing navigation on icons can in some cases result in bad UX, since some users may misunderstand the meaning of an icon when there is no explanatory caption.

This design has the menu title in focus and places the icon behind the text. The animations is simple and is therefore not occupying too much of the users' attention.
