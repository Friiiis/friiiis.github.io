Case:

Designing the logo and brand of a fitness startup. Additionally the client needed business cards and stickers.

The client wanted the logo to have a feeling of being healthy and to visibly show that it was a fitness brand.

The final product is an abstract illustration of a person holding two dumbbells. The person's body is a twisted line representing the Caduceus which is often used in logos for hospitals and other health logos.
